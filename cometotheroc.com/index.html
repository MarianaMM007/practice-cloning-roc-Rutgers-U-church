ROC 2.0 is a NJ non-denominational Christian church. All are welcome!

Our goal is to compassionately share Jesus and His love with hurting and hungry Rutgers students and folks of all ages in the surrounding New Brunswick, Piscataway, North Brunswick, Somerset, Franklin, East Brunswick, and Edison areas. Our contemporary nondenominational church gathering is located at the epicenter of Rutgers University at our location at 100 College Ave in New Brunswick
